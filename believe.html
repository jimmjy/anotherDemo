Gone are the days of hammering dull messages into captive television audiences’ minds. The jig is up on banner ads, and the most viewed part of a pre-roll ad is almost always the countdown clock. Traditional canvases are vanishing left, right and center. But far too many agencies, and marketers, are still marching to the beat of the old drum.
Juliet exists to set the old drum on fire. We exist to tell more interesting stories in more interesting places, to help brands find ways to add real value to people’s lives, and to recognize that context is now as important as creativity itself. It’s why we’re made up of a unique combination of people from creative, media, consulting, and art who all share one key trait: The will to chase what could be. Even if it kills us.
